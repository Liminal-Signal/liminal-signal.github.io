# Op-Ed pitch guidelines

Your Op-Ed pitch should be clear, timely, and tied to a technical angle you can defend.

## Highlights

- State the claim in one sentence
- List 2-3 sources you plan to cite
- Explain why the topic matters now

## Action items

- Draft a 150-200 word pitch
- Add it to your weekly post
- Request feedback from an editor
