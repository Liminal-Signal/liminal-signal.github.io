# Op-Ed inspiration

Op-Eds connect technical work to real people. Look for stories where computing changes outcomes.

## Highlights

- Focus on one clear argument
- Ground the story with a real example
- End with a concrete next step

## Action items

- Collect two possible topics
- Sketch a headline and thesis
- Bring one idea to workshop
